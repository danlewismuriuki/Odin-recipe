lasagna-Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a baked pasta dish of Italian origin made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and b√©chamel sauce. The dish is typically baked in an oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Lasagna is a popular dish in Italy and around the world. It is often served as a main course or as a part of a larger meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 (15-ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 (10-ounce) package frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
  • 1 (15-ounce) container shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain off any excess grease.
  3. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  5. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, spinach, and 1/2 cup of the mozzarella cheese.
  6. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Top with 3 lasagna noodles, overlapping as needed.
  8. Spread with half of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  9. Top with 1 cup of the meat sauce.
  10. Repeat layers.
  11. Top with the remaining 3 lasagna noodles.
  12. Spread with the remaining meat sauce.
  13. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  14. Bake for 30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  15. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
